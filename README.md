# OwnForge

> Your AI. Your Data. Your Forge.

**OwnForge** is a self-hosted, AI-native personal productivity suite.
One Docker Compose, one account, your Notes / Chat / Bookmarks — all running on your own machine, powered by your own AI (Ollama / OpenAI / Claude, your choice).

🚧 **Status**: Early development. Phase 1 in progress. Not yet usable.

## Why OwnForge

- **Own** your data — everything stays on your disk / NAS / VPS.
- **Own** your AI — bring your own API key or run Ollama locally.
- **One suite, not ten apps** — Notes, Chat, and more share SSO, UI, and data.

## Open Source Promise

OwnForge will **always** be released under an OSI-approved open source license.
Core functionality will **always** be free, open, and self-hostable.

A future managed hosting service may exist as a convenience offering, but it will never be the only way to use OwnForge.
